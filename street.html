Meet the Honda NX500, the latest evolution in adventure biking. Replacing the iconic CB500X, the NX500 combines cutting-edge technology, rugged style, and Honda’s renowned reliability. Whether navigating winding roads or exploring off-road trails, the NX500 is designed to take you further, faster, and more confidently than ever before. With enhanced handling, lighter wheels, and advanced rider assistance systems, this mini adventurer is your perfect companion for any journey.

Features and Style

Performance

The NX500 is powered by a 471cc parallel twin-cylinder engine, delivering 35kW and 43Nm of torque. With Honda Selectable Torque Control (HSTC) and improved ECU settings, acceleration is smooth and responsive, giving you full control across varied terrains.

Bodywork

With new cast aluminum wheels and an overall 3kg weight reduction compared to its predecessor, the NX500 is more agile and easier to handle, ensuring an enjoyable ride every time.

Safety

Dual 296mm front discs with Nissin axial-mounted calipers and ABS provide responsive braking power, while the LED headlight and taillight ensure maximum visibility day and night.
