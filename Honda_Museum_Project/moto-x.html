CRF250R is stronger than ever, gaining the MXGP championship-winning chassis of the 22YM CRF450R, plus extensive cylinder head development for a considerable low-rpm torque boost. Find a dealer and book a test ride now!

Features and Style

Performance

Low-rpm combustion stability and gas flow in, and out, of the chamber served as main focus. eadlines are a revised air intake funnel and cone tube, fed by a 78% larger- capacity airbox, now 4.1L, an injector angle now set at 60° (rather than 30°) and a straight exhaust port.Air intake efficiency is improved, alongside air intake cooling. The air filter’s also easier to access.

Control

Three-mode HRC Launch Control helps any rider – from beginner to expert – hit the mark straight out of the gates, every time!

Design

Standard-fit, lightweight Renthal Fatbar flex for optimal comfort; the top yoke features two handlebar-holder locations for moving the handlebar rearward and forward by 26mm. When the holder is turned 180°, the handlebar can be moved an additional 10mm from the base position, resulting in four unique riding positions.
