our treatment

stone massage

uses smooth, heated stones placed on specific points of the body. The heat helps to relax muscles, improve circulation, and relieve tension. Cold stones may also be used to reduce inflammation and stimulate energy flow.

body massage

Body massage involves manipulating muscles and soft tissues to promote relaxation and improve well-being. It helps reduce stress, alleviate pain, and enhance circulation. Common types include Swedish, deep tissue, and hot stone massages.

Herbal treatment

Herbal treatment uses plant-based remedies to improve health and treat various conditions. It includes herbs, roots, and flowers in forms like teas and tinctures. Often part of traditional medicine, it complement modern healthcare.
